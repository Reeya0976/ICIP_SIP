Parliament Monsoon Session Live Updates: AAP’s Sanjay Singh suspended from Rajya Sabha for Monsoon Session


Parliament Monsoon Session Day 3 Live Updates (July 24): As ruckus in the Rajya Sabha over the Manipur issue today, Chairman Jagdeep Dhankar suspended AAP MP Sanjay Singh for the entire duration of the Monsoon session for “repeatedly violating the directives of the Chair”. Sanjay Singh was seen in the Well of the House before being suspended. In the continuing standoff between the Opposition and the government on the Manipur issue, both Houses of the Parliament were adjourned today. The Rajya Sabha was adjourned till 2 pm, and the Lok Sabha till 2 pm. While the audio was not clear on what TMC MP Derek O’Brien said, Chairman Jagdeep Dhankar reprimanded him, saying, “You are challenging the Chair.” Shortly after this, amid ruckus, the Upper House was adjourned. In Lok Sabha, Speaker Om Birla said the House were ready to have a discussion on the Manipur issue, but the Opposition does not get to decide who responds. MPs from the Opposition-front INDIA and the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) staged separate protests near the Gandhi Statue in the Parliament today: while the Opposition demanded Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s statement on the Manipur violence in Parliament, the BJP MPs protested the alleged rise in atrocities and crimes against women in Rajasthan.
